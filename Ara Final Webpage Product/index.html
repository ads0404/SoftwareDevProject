ICT Sector

The Information and Communication Technology (ICT) sector at Ara Institute of Canterbury offers a range of programs designed to prepare students for successful careers in the tech industry. Our programs are developed in collaboration with industry partners to ensure they are current and relevant.

Courses Available

  • Software Development
  • Networking
  • Cyber Security
  • Data Science
  • Web Development

Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert faculty provide students with the knowledge and hands-on experience needed to excel in the ICT field. Additionally, our strong industry connections offer students numerous opportunities for internships and employment after graduation.
